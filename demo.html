--- title: Demo description: Play around and explore freely the Decidim platform with our demo ---

Decidim is up and running on several instances that you can visit and explore, Decidim Barcelona at Barcelona City or you can visit the sites of Gava and Hospitalet. But these are real instances where you can't really play around freely, you might not a citizen of those municipalities, and you can't explore those sites as an admin.

If you want to go further you are welcome to test Decidim on our Decidim Demo Site (courtesy of our lead developers at Codegram). Enter the demo site and freely explore the potential of Decidim and its features. You can either log in as a regular user (with username "user@example.org" and password "decidim123456") or you can login and explore as and Admin (username "admin@example.org" and password "decidim123456").
